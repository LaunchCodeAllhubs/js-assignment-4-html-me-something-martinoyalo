A brief history of soccer

Before the 1800s, there were a few games around the world that centered around kicking ot juggling a ball

In the 1860s a group of people in England got together the form the rules that set up a game where using hands were illegal and several other rules somewhat resembling the rules of taday.

In 1904, Federation Internationale de Football Association(FIFA) was born. They set up an international competition in 1930 with the inaguaral Fifa World Cup

Since then a few changes have been made including a rule not allowing the goalkeeper to pick the ball up after a teammate passes it to them.

Today the sport is played by many people all around the world.
